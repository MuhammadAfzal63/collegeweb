ADMISSIONS

ADMISSION POLICY/PROCEDURE

1. Only male students are eligible to get admission in intermediate and degree classes.

2. Fresh students will be preferred.

3. Students seeking admission at various level will buy a prospectus from the college office.
After reading it carefully, they will choose their subject and fill the admission form. They will
complete their documents and submit the complete file in office and  get a receipt.
