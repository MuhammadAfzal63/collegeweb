Message of The Principal


Dear Student,

Govt. Associate College Tranda Muhammad Pannah started its glorious educational traditions and activities in August, 2019. Before the establishment of the College, students of Tranda Muhammad Pannah had no College to move on their education after their matriculation but now, students are ready to serve their motherland in high and influential position and bringing it the good name with their unmatched abilities. College has beautiful building a grassy ground, 12 class rooms, eight labs, principal residence, hostel for teachers and C-4 and garages for buses.


We are doing our best to carry on the continuity of best educational activities. Our aim is to follow the merit policy, saying no to any compromise on merit and the quality of education. We are proud to declare that we have the best teaching faculty who are imparting education and training to the youth with their best abilities, bringing forth their best in academic, mental and moral aspects of their personality. We feel honor to say that we are producing the best academic results.


HEC has launched ADP semester in the variety of disciplines which is being offered to the students. You are welcomed with full faith and confidence that it will groom your personality to shine as a star on the horizon of intellect, wisdom and prosperity. We are sanguine that you will feel honored and delighted to have studied in the Great alma mater of higher learning known as Govt. Associate College Tranda Muhammad Pannah.


Prof. Qazi Muhammad Ayub

Principal
