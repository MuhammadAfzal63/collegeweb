Rules and Regulations


Attendance


  • Students are required to maintain regular attendance, and their attendance percentage
    should not fall below 75%. This is a mandatory requirement for promotion to the next class.
    Failure to meet this attendance requirement will result in students not being allowed to
    appear for board examinations in accordance with the rules.
  • Students can request a maximum of 5 days of leave in a month.
  • If a student is absent continuously or irregularly for 10 days in a month, his name will be
    removed from the college roll. Parents will be required to meet with the principal on an
    assigned date. Re-admission will be considered only once, following the rules. Admission to the
    board examinations may also be withheld due to insufficient attendance.

Category / Discipline


  • Incomplete uniform: Fine of Rs. 100
  • Without uniform: Fine of Rs. 150
  • Breach of any discipline: Fine ranging from Rs. 200 to Rs. 1000
  • Bringing a camera, tape recorder, or cellular phone to the college: A fine of Rs. 1000 will be charged.
    The items will only be returned upon the written request of the student's parents.

Leave Application


  • A booklet containing leave forms is available at the time of admission.
  • Leave applications for up to 5 days must be approved by the subject teacher before being
    submitted to the leave incharge.
  • Leave applications for up to 15 days must be approved by the leave incharge only.
  • For leave lasting longer than 15 days, the vice principal's approval is required.
  • Applications for leave of 6 or more days due to illness must be accompanied by a medical certificate.
  • Students suffering from infectious diseases must obtain an immunity certificate before rejoining the college.
  • Students who are absent from the college for 10 days without proper leave approval
    from the designated authority will be automatically dropped from the college roll.
  • Students must maintain a minimum attendance of 75% as required by the board rules.

Parent-Teacher Meeting


  • Parent-teacher meetings will be held twice a year, following the December tests and promotion examinations.

COLLEGE UNIFORM/IDENTITY CARD

COLLEGE UNIFORM

No student is allowed to enter in the college premises without uniform.

FIRST YEAR TO FOURTH YEAR

  • White shirt with coller and a front pocket
  • (Pants) Dark Grey (Shalwar Kameez is not allowed)
  • Neck Tie (Maroon), Socks & Shoes (Black)
  • Coat/Sweater/Jersy (Maroon)

COLLEGE IDENTITY CARD

After getting admission in the College, students must keep college ID Card with them all the time. Students can get their ID Card Form Examination Department showing their original challan voucher. No student would be allowed to enter in the college without college ID card.
